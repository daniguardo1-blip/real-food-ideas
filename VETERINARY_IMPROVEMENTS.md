# Veterinary-Grade Pet Care Report System

## Overview

The Pet Care Report generation system has been upgraded with professional, veterinary-grade content that prioritizes animal safety and follows evidence-based best practices.

---

## ✅ Key Improvements

### 1. **Professional System Prompt**

The report generation now follows a structured veterinary nutritionist approach:

- **Evidence-based recommendations** - All advice based on veterinary science
- **Safety-first approach** - Never recommends unsafe foods or practices
- **Species-specific guidance** - Tailored advice for each animal type
- **Professional tone** - Written as expert pet care advisor, not AI

---

### 2. **New Section: Forbidden Foods**

A critical safety feature has been added to every Pet Care Report.

#### What It Does

- Lists foods that are **toxic or dangerous** for the specific pet type
- Explains **why each food is dangerous** with specific health risks
- Provides **clear warnings** to keep foods away from pets
- Adapts dynamically based on animal species

#### Species Coverage

The system provides forbidden foods lists for:

- **Dogs** (10 dangerous foods)
- **Cats** (10 dangerous foods)
- **Fish** (5 dangerous items)
- **Birds** (9 dangerous foods)
- **Rabbits** (8 dangerous foods)
- **Reptiles** (7 dangerous items)
- **Other pets** (5 general dangerous foods)

---

## 🐕 Forbidden Foods Examples

### For Dogs

The system warns about:

- **Chocolate** - Contains theobromine, causes seizures, heart problems, death
- **Grapes & Raisins** - Acute kidney failure, even in small amounts
- **Onions & Garlic** - Damage red blood cells, cause anemia
- **Xylitol** - Rapid insulin release, hypoglycemia, liver failure
- **Macadamia Nuts** - Weakness, vomiting, tremors, hyperthermia
- **Alcohol** - Vomiting, breathing problems, coma, death
- **Caffeine** - Hyperactivity, rapid heart rate, seizures, death
- **Avocado** - Persin causes vomiting and diarrhea
- **Cooked Bones** - Splintering, choking, internal injuries
- **Raw Dough** - Stomach expansion, alcohol production

### For Cats

The system warns about:

- **Chocolate** - Theobromine toxicity, heart problems, seizures
- **Onions & Garlic** - Hemolytic anemia from red blood cell destruction
- **Grapes & Raisins** - Kidney failure
- **Alcohol** - Severe intoxication, coma, death
- **Caffeine** - Restlessness, rapid breathing, palpitations, seizures
- **Xylitol** - Liver failure, severe hypoglycemia
- **Dairy Products** - Lactose intolerance, digestive upset
- **Raw Fish** - Thiaminase enzyme destroys vitamin B1
- **Bones** - Splintering, choking, intestinal blockage
- **Dog Food** - Lacks taurine, essential nutrients for cats

### For Birds

The system warns about:

- **Avocado** - Persin is EXTREMELY toxic to birds - causes heart damage, death
- **Chocolate** - Theobromine causes seizures and death
- **Caffeine** - Causes cardiac arrest and hyperactivity
- **Alcohol** - Organ failure
- **Salt** - Excessive thirst, dehydration, kidney dysfunction, death
- **Onions & Garlic** - Hemolytic anemia, digestive upset
- **Apple Seeds** - Contain cyanide compounds
- **Raw Beans** - Contain hemagglutinin toxin
- **Fruit Pits** - Cyanide and choking hazards

### For Fish

The system warns about:

- **Bread & Baked Goods** - Bloating, digestive issues, no nutrition
- **Human Processed Foods** - High salt, preservatives, additives
- **Meat & Chicken** - Not nutritionally balanced, pollutes water
- **Fruits & Vegetables** - Most species cannot digest properly
- **Overfeeding** - Obesity and severe water quality degradation

### For Rabbits

The system warns about:

- **Iceberg Lettuce** - Contains lactucarium causing diarrhea
- **Beans & Legumes** - Gas and painful bloating
- **Potatoes** - Solanine toxin, especially when green/sprouted
- **Chocolate** - Seizures and death
- **Sugary Foods** - Disrupts gut bacteria, severe digestive problems
- **Dairy Products** - Cannot digest lactose
- **Nuts & Seeds** - High fat, digestive upset
- **Processed Human Foods** - Harmful additives, excessive salt/sugar

### For Reptiles

The system warns about:

- **Avocado** - Toxic to many reptile species
- **Rhubarb** - Contains toxic oxalic acid
- **Fireflies/Lightning Bugs** - EXTREMELY toxic - can kill reptiles quickly
- **Wild-Caught Insects** - May carry pesticides and parasites
- **Processed Human Foods** - Lack proper nutrients, harmful additives
- **Dairy Products** - Cannot digest lactose
- **Spinach & Kale (excess)** - High oxalates bind calcium

## 🛡️ Safety Guidelines Implemented

### Dietary Safety Rules

The system now enforces these critical safety rules:

✅ **Never recommend raw poultry** - Bacterial risks (Salmonella, E. coli)

✅ **No toxic foods** - Comprehensive forbidden foods database

✅ **No laser toys as primary activity for cats** - Can cause frustration and obsessive behavior

✅ **Prefer balanced commercial foods** - From reputable brands with proper nutrition

✅ **Avoid controversial advice** - Only evidence-based recommendations

✅ **No medical diagnoses** - Always recommends veterinary consultation for health issues

---

### Species-Specific Guidelines

#### Cats (Obligate Carnivores)

- **Require taurine** - Essential amino acid
- **Avoid high carbohydrates** - Not natural for cats
- **Prefer high-protein wet/dry food**
- **Suggested activities:** Interactive toys, hunting toys, climbing structures

#### Dogs

- **Regular outdoor activity** - Essential for physical and mental health
- **Balanced protein levels** - Age and breed appropriate
- **Activities:** Walking, fetch, enrichment games, socialization

#### Fish

- **Aquarium health focus** - Water quality is critical
- **Species-appropriate food** - Different species have different needs
- **Activities:** Aquarium enrichment, proper habitat care

#### Birds

- **Mental stimulation essential** - Cage enrichment critical
- **Balanced diet:** Seed/pellet mix plus fresh foods
- **Activities:** Foraging toys, social interaction, flight opportunities

#### Rabbits

- **Unlimited timothy hay** - 80% of diet
- **Limited pellets** - 1/4 cup per 5 lbs body weight
- **Fresh vegetables daily** - Introduce slowly
- **Activities:** Digging boxes, tunnels, chew toys

#### Reptiles

- **Species-specific diets** - Carnivorous vs. herbivorous
- **Calcium/D3 supplements** - Essential for bone health
- **Temperature regulation** - Critical for digestion and health
- **Activities:** Proper basking areas, hiding spots, climbing structures
